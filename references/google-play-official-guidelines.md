## Policy-Critical Rules

1. No misleading, irrelevant, or excessive metadata.
2. No fake/incentivized ratings, reviews, or installs.
3. No deceptive promotion and no forced-install style traffic.
4. No spammy repetition, cloned low-value patterns, or keyword abuse.

## Operational Limits

- App title: 30 characters
- Short description: 80 characters
- Full description: 4000 characters

## Growth Features (Compliant)

- Custom Store Listings: up to 50 listing variants
- Store listing experiments: icon, graphics, and localized tests
- Ranking model includes quality and user relevance signals

## Implementation Note

Optimization recommendations must include both:

- Conversion lift objective
- Policy risk check per proposed change
