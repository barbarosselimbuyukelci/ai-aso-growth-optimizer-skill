## Policy-Critical Rules

1. Metadata must reflect real app functionality and experience.
2. Misleading claims and deceptive creatives are disallowed.
3. Discovery fraud is prohibited (chart/search/review manipulation).
4. Fake or incentivized reviews/ratings are high-risk violations.

## Operational Limits (App Store Connect)

- App Name: 30 characters
- Subtitle: 30 characters
- Description: 4000 characters
- Keywords: 100 bytes

## Growth Features (Compliant)

- Custom Product Pages: up to 70 variants per app
- Product Page Optimization: controlled A/B style testing with confidence reporting
- In-App Events: extra discovery surface for seasonal and event-driven demand
